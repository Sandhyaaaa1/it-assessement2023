Welcome to Leo Club of Kathmandu Lord Buddha

Leo Club of Kathmandu, the first Leo Club in Kathmandu and second in the country, Nepal, was established in 1972 in the initiation of 50 young leaders of the society. The club was brought into foundation as inspired from the slogan ‘WE SERVE’, to create and foster a spirit of understanding among all people for humanitarian needs by providing voluntary services throughout community involvement and international co-operation.
